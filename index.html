Fight to save Ivalice kingdom

Final Fantasy: Dawn of Ivalice is an Action RPG game based on Final Fantasy Tactics Advance. In Dawn of Ivalice, our protagonists Marche, Ritz and Shara open a book that redirects them to Ivalice Kingdom, where the terrible creatures from the Darkness have invaded the entire kingdom. Marche, Ritz and Shara must join forces to defeat evil and cleanse Ivalice's kingdom from all the invading darkness to return from the world that they knew.

In Dawn of Ivalice, each character is focused on a different class with unique abilities. The player will have the freedom to change between the characters at all times to combine their skills and develop different strategies according to the situation that is presented.

 
 

Combine your Heroes

Marche

Marche

Formidable warrior with great courage and determination. His ability kit is focused on area attacks and is very useful in situations when the enemy surrounds you.

Ritz

Ritz

Wise and intelligent magician. She has a great destructive power thanks to her spells and her skill kit is very useful to evade enemies.

Shara

Shara

Agile archer with great dexterity in his hands. Her skill kit is focused to generate damage from long distances and inflict damage per second.
